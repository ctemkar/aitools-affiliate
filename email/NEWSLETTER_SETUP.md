# Email Newsletter Setup - AI Tools Affiliate

## 🎯 Newsletter Purpose
1. **Build audience** for affiliate program applications
2. **Establish authority** in AI tools space
3. **Drive traffic** to new reviews/content
4. **Generate revenue** through affiliate links
5. **Collect feedback** from engaged audience

## 📧 Email Service Provider Options

### **Option 1: ConvertKit (Recommended)**
- **Price**: Free up to 1,000 subscribers, then $29/month
- **Pros**: Best for creators, easy affiliate integration
- **Cons**: More expensive at scale
- **Affiliate Program**: Yes (30% recurring)

### **Option 2: Beehiiv**
- **Price**: Free starter plan, $42/month Grow plan
- **Pros**: Built-in monetization, great analytics
- **Cons**: Newer platform, fewer integrations
- **Affiliate Program**: Yes

### **Option 3: MailerLite**
- **Price**: Free up to 1,000 subscribers, $10/month after
- **Pros**: Cheapest, good features
- **Cons**: Less creator-focused
- **Affiliate Program**: Limited

### **Option 4: Substack**
- **Price**: Free (they take 10% if you charge)
- **Pros**: Built-in audience, easy setup
- **Cons**: Less control, their branding
- **Affiliate Program**: Through paid subscriptions

## 📝 Recommended: ConvertKit
**Why**: Best affiliate program, creator-friendly, scales well

## 📊 Growth Strategy

### **Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1)**
- **Goal**: 100 subscribers
- **Methods**:
  1. Website signup form (primary)
  2. Social media promotion
  3. Content upgrades (PDF guides)
  4. Personal network outreach

### **Phase 2: Growth (Months 2-3)**
- **Goal**: 500 subscribers
- **Methods**:
  1. Guest posting with bio link
  2. Social media collaborations
  3. Webinar signups
  4. Referral program

### **Phase 3: Scale (Months 4-6)**
- **Goal**: 2,000 subscribers
- **Methods**:
  1. Paid advertising (small budget)
  2. Co-marketing with AI tools
  3. Content partnerships
  4. SEO-optimized lead magnets

## 📈 Monetization Timeline

### **Month 1-2**: Build audience (no direct monetization)

### **Month 3**: Begin affiliate promotions

- Clear disclosure, honest reviews
- Track clicks/conversions

### **Month 4-6**: Scale revenue
