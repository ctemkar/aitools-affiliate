    • What is Claude?

    Claude is Anthropic's AI assistant, known for its strong ethical framework, large context window, and excellent writing capabilities. It's designed to be helpful, harmless, and honest.

    1. **Large Context Window** - 200K tokens (150K+ words)

    2. **File Uploads** - Analyze PDFs, Word docs, Excel, PowerPoint

    3. **Web Search** - Access current information (Pro feature)

    4. **Code Generation** - Supports 50+ programming languages

    5. **Artifact Creation** - Generate and edit code, documents, spreadsheets

    6. **Team Collaboration** - Share conversations and artifacts

    7. **API Access** - Integrate Claude into your applications

    8. **Constitutional AI** - Built with safety and ethics in mind
